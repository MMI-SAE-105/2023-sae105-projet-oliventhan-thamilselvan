Glossary

Feminism :

Social and political movement that aims to establish gender equality and promote women's rights in society.

Suffragettes :

Women activists from the early 20th century who campaigned for women's right to vote.

#MeToo :

Movement against sexual harassment and sexual assault, popularised in 2017.

Time's Up :

Movement launched by entertainment industry professionals in response to the #MeToo movement and allegations of abuse in Hollywood.

Line :

A sentence or dialogue spoken by a character in a film or play.

Cultural Icon :

A symbol or figure that is widely recognised or respected in a culture or sub-culture because of its importance or significance.

Narrative :

A coherent, organised structure of events and statements, often used to tell a story or describe an event.

Authenticity :

The quality of something that is real, true or original without pretence or artifice.

Societal :

Relating to the structure, organisation or operation of the company.

Femininity :

A set of characteristics or qualities traditionally associated with women, such as gentleness, sensitivity and tenderness.
