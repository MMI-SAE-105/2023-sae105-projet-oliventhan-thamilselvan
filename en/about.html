About

Welcome to Impossible To Be Her, a space dedicated to celebrating and exploring the many facets of feminism through the prism of film and popular culture.

Site creator

Oliventhan Thamilselvan

MMi 1st year - Group B2

Founded in 2023, our site is inspired by the line "It's literally impossible to be a woman", reflecting the challenges and triumphs that women face every day. We firmly believe that film, music and literature have the power not only to entertain, but also to raise awareness, educate and inspire change.

Here is a micro-trottoir carried out as part of our studies to show you the range of skills covered in this course.

This project was carried out as part of an educational exercise at the MMI department in Montbéliard.
